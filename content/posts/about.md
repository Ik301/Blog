## Who Am I?

Princeton class of 2029, Financial Engineering major and Philsophy minor. Passionate about variety of things like poetry, philsophy, technology, and more. Navigating life as best as I can, enjoying all it has to offer.  
  
I started this blog in 2022 as a personal project, an online notebook of sorts. A place to share my thoughts, passions, and findings. I hope you join me in my journey, growing along side me!

## Schedule

New blog posts will be shared at least once a month. I write my academic and technology blog posts based on current interests/research. My book summaries come as I finish new books, and I write posts on my extracurriculars as I get involved in new things. Poetry is my favorite hobby, but I write as I’m inspired, which may take days or months. If you want to get updated whenever I post, subscribe to my blog!
