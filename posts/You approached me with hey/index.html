You Approached Me With Hey

January 17, 2025

#Poetry #Love #Longing Why do I still long for your touch

Hearing your voice, Now an echo of past

Waiting your text, One never to come

Have I not cried, Stifled by plight

Have you not hurt, Me long enough

I long to forget, Yet brand your name

Til the day You say "hey"
