i had a dream

September 21, 2025 • 1 min read

***

what is a revolution? a, revolution is

to fathom its heights, dare not. yet scale with hope, a faith?

assured of things, hope becomes faith.

dangerous, that faith where was it placed?

but I smelled it, kneaded a dozen. but I felt it, a dry cotton.

but I need it, a, mirage.

let me chase it, something, a, hope though false.

you say, the revolt has failed.

existed— a, paradox. —never.

beyond, lies, I saw.

a, to build atop the wall we climbed.

to have fallen, before zenith, maybe a blessing for the faithful.

a, vanity.
